What are Xsolla Points?

Xsolla Points are a virtual currency that converts into discounts to purchase games and add-ons. You can earn Xsolla Points by completing various activities on Xsolla platforms.

How to spend Xsolla Points?

You can spend your Xsolla Points to get a discount while making a purchase with Xsolla. To do so, log in to Xsolla Pay during the purchase process. A corresponding Xsolla Points block will be displayed during checkout if your merchant supports Xsolla Points. The discount will be applied automatically....

How to earn Xsolla Points?

Xsolla Points are rewarded to users by completing the activities on Offerwall. Points are generally awarded to users 24 hours after activity.
